# Commercial Use of cross-ai-core

cross-ai-core is released under the [MIT License](LICENSE), which permits free use for
personal, academic, research, and open-source projects.

## When a commercial license applies

Organizations deploying cross-ai-core in the following contexts are encouraged to contact
us for a commercial license and support agreement:

- **Commercial use** — any deployment within a for-profit business, including
  internal use, client work, or integration into a product or service
- **Government use** — federal, state, local, or international government agencies
  and contractors
- **Media organizations** — news outlets, publishers, or content platforms using
  cross-ai-core in an editorial or production workflow
- **SaaS or hosted services** — offering cross-ai-core or its output as part of a
  subscription or paid service

## What a commercial license includes

- Written authorization for organizational deployment
- Priority support and direct access to the author
- Consulting and integration services (available separately)
- A relationship for long-term development aligned with your use case

## Why this matters

cross-ai-core is free to use today because broad adoption is the goal. When organizations
deploy cross-ai-core to dispatch AI providers and generate content at scale — particularly
in commercial or government contexts — a licensing relationship ensures the project is
sustainable and that you have a direct support path.
